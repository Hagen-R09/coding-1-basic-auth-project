=========================
APP PLANNING & DESIGN
=========================

1. PROJECT NAME & THEME
-------------------------
- What is your app called, and what type of app is it?  notsey, a notation app
- What style or vibe do you want users to experience? an artistic welcoming vibe, as I want the app to focus on art

2. CORE FUNCTIONALITY
-------------------------
- What are the essential features your app must have?  an ability to create notes similar to google docs or the notes app
- What optional features could make your app more interactive or personalized? sketch notes and image importation

3. SECRET OR PERSONALIZED CONTENT

-------------------------

- Will this content change depending on who is using the app (admin, user, special user?) likely not

-------------------------

- How will users navigate between pages? tabbing and or files and folders like google docs and drive

-------------------------
